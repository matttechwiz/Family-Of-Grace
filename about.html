About Family of Grace

Discover our mission, vision, and the values that unite us as one family in Christ.

Who We Are

At Family of Grace, we are a Christ-centered community passionate about raising believers grounded in the Word and empowered by grace. Rooted in faith, hope, and love, we exist to glorify God and bring transformation to lives and families across the world.

Our Mission

To nurture disciples of Christ who live out God’s Word, build strong families, and impact society with love and service.

Our Vision

He made known to us the mystery of His will according to His good pleasure, which He purposed in Christ, to be put into effect when the times reach their fulfillment—to bring unity to all things in heaven and on earth under Christ. Ephesians 1:9–10 (NIV)

Our Core Values

  • Faith in Christ
  • Love & Compassion
  • Service to Community
  • Spiritual Growth
  • Unity in the Body of Christ

Our Story

Family of Grace was founded by Rev’d Zenith Nwebo, a passionate servant of God dedicated to spreading the gospel and raising a generation of believers empowered by grace. With branches across Nigeria and a growing global family, we continue to impact lives with God’s Word and love.

Our Community

We welcome people of all ages and walks of life—families, youths, children, and individuals—into a fellowship where they can grow, belong, and serve.
